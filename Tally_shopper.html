Shopper 9 Introduction

Shopper 9 PoS along with Shopper 9 HO and Shopper 9 distributor, is up to task to meet the unique challenges of every retail business. Shopper 9 addresses all point of sale requirements such as billing, discounts and schemes, price management, MIS reporting and also common issues such as data exchange between head office, warehouses and stores.

Retail Features of shopper 9

The requirements of a retail business vary. Shopper 9 supports an out-of-the box capability for you to configure and run it as per your business requirements. Configure Shopper 9 to effectively reduce errors and increases customer satisfaction. Retail businesses of all sizes, dealing with different products are benefited by Shopper 9’s ease of deployment, flexible architecture, and intuitive interface.
